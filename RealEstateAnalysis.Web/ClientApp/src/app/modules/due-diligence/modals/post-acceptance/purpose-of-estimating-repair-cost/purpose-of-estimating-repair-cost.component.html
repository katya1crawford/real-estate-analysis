Purpose of Estimating Repair Cost

One of the purposes of this entire inspection process is to put an estimated cost to each item in the list and arrive at a total number that you can take back to the seller and discuss. If you bring these issues to the seller’s attention in most case they will be fixed before you close, or you will get reduction in price. That save you money and in some cases, lots of it.

Do not only include cost of repairs into price reduction, but also include lost revenue from units being down for however long before they are rented.

Use discovered deferred maintenance and needed repairs information in deciding to keep or fire existing property management company, if it’s managed by one.
